Character A

Dark-Haired Fit Gym Friend

Voice: Will - Relaxed Optimist

  • Keep dark wavy volume, tan skin, defined jawline, and calm confident expression.
  • Wardrobe stays black sleeveless tank, black shorts, white crew socks, white trainers, thin chain.
  • No beard, hat, sunglasses, tattoos added, bulky redesign, or outfit color change.

Character B

Blond Strength Gym Friend

Voice: Chris - Charming, Down-to-Earth

  • Keep short curly blond hair, light stubble, sturdy build, and grounded practical expression.
  • Wardrobe stays loose charcoal gray T-shirt, olive shorts, black smartwatch, white socks, white trainers.
  • No clean-shaven redesign, fitted tank replacement, beard, hat, sunglasses, tattoos added, or outfit color change.
